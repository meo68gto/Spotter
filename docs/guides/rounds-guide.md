# Rounds Guide: Scheduling Games

Complete guide to creating, joining, and managing golf rounds on Spotter.

## Overview

Rounds are at the heart of Spotter. Whether you're creating a round for your network or joining someone else's game, this guide covers everything you need to know.

---

## Understanding Round Tiers

### Who Can Create Rounds?

| Tier | Create Rounds | Monthly Limit |
|------|---------------|---------------|
| **FREE** | ❌ | N/A |
| **SELECT** | ✅ | 4 per month |
| **SUMMIT** | ✅ | Unlimited |

### Who Can Join Rounds?

**All tiers** can join rounds:
- FREE members: Join any public round
- SELECT/SUMMIT: Join any round (public or private)

## Joining Rounds

### Finding Rounds to Join

#### Browse Available Rounds

1. Open the **Golf** tab
2. Tap **"Find Rounds"**
3. Browse by:
   - **Date**: When you want to play
   - **Location**: Courses near you
   - **Format**: Stroke play, match play, etc.

#### Filter Options

Refine your search:
- **Date range**: Next 7, 14, or 30 days
- **Distance**: Within 10, 25, or 50 miles
- **Format**: Stroke play, scramble, etc.
- **Spots available**: At least 1, 2, or 3 spots
- **Course**: Specific courses you prefer

### Understanding Round Details

Before joining, review:

**Course Information:**
- Name and location
- Difficulty rating
- Amenities available

**Round Details:**
- Date and tee time
- Format (stroke play, etc.)
- Total spots vs. available
- Organizer reputation score

**Participant Info:**
- Who's already joined
- Their handicaps
- Mutual connections

### Joining Process

#### Public Rounds

1. Find a round you like
2. Tap **"Join Round"**
3. Confirm your spot
4. Receive confirmation
5. Check your inbox for round chat

**What happens:**
- You're immediately added (if spots available)
- Round chat thread created
- Organizer notified
- Reminders scheduled

#### Private Rounds

1. Receive invite code from organizer
2. Go to **Golf → Join Private Round**
3. Enter invite code
4. Tap **"Join"**
5. Wait for organizer approval (if required)

**Note:** Private rounds may require organizer approval.

### After Joining

**Immediate actions:**
- Introduce yourself in round chat
- Confirm tee time and meeting spot
- Ask about payment (if applicable)
- Check course requirements (dress code, etc.)

**Pre-round reminders:**
- 24 hours before: Confirm attendance
- Day of: Check weather, arrive early
- Post-round: Mark complete, rate experience

## Creating Rounds (SELECT+)

### Planning Your Round

#### Step 1: Choose Course

Browse or search:
- **Favorites**: Courses you've played
- **Nearby**: Within your preferred distance
- **Search**: By name, city, or features

#### Step 2: Select Date/Time

Consider:
- **Your schedule**: When you're free
- **Popular times**: Weekend mornings fill fast
- **Weather**: Check forecast
- **Course availability**: Book in advance

#### Step 3: Set Format

Choose your format:

| Format | Best For |
|--------|----------|
| **Stroke Play** | Competitive, scoring |
| **Match Play** | Head-to-head competition |
| **Scramble** | Team building, mixed skills |
| **Shamble** | Mix of scramble and individual |
| **Best Ball** | Team scoring |

#### Step 4: Determine Spots

How many golfers:
- **2 spots**: One-on-one, focused
- **3 spots**: Small group, flexible
- **4 spots**: Standard foursome
- **5+ spots**: Multiple groups, tournament style

#### Step 5: Privacy Settings

**Public Round:**
- Visible to same-tier members
- Anyone can join (while spots available)
- Good for meeting new people

**Private Round:**
- Invite-only via code
- You control who joins
- Good for established groups

**Require Approval:**
- Members request to join
- You approve/decline
- Good for screening participants

### Creating the Round

#### In the App

1. Go to **Golf** tab
2. Tap **"+"** or **"Create Round"**
3. Select course
4. Set date and time
5. Choose format
6. Set number of spots
7. Configure privacy
8. Add notes (optional)
9. Tap **"Create Round"**

#### Round Notes Tips

Write helpful notes:
- **Skill level**: "Looking for 10-20 handicaps"
- **Expectations**: "Competitive but fun"
- **Logistics**: "Meet at clubhouse 15 min early"
- **Payment**: "$85 green fee, pay at course"

### Managing Your Round

#### Invite Members (Private Rounds)

1. Open your round
2. Tap **"Invite"**
3. Share via:
   - **Copy code**: Manual sharing
   - **Message**: Send to connections
   - **Social**: Share outside Spotter

#### Approve Join Requests

If requiring approval:
1. Check **"Pending Requests"**
2. Review requestor's profile
3. Tap **"Approve"** or **"Decline"**
4. Add message (optional)

**Screening criteria:**
- Handicap compatibility
- Reputation score
- Past round history
- Mutual connections

#### Managing Participants

**Before the round:**
- Send reminders
- Confirm headcount
- Share logistics
- Handle cancellations

**Day of:**
- Welcome everyone
- Organize tee assignments
- Collect any payments
- Take group photo

**After:**
- Thank participants
- Mark round complete
- Share scores
- Plan next round

### Round Limits (SELECT Tier)

**Monthly quota:** 4 rounds

**Resets:** First of each month

**What counts:**
- ✅ Rounds created (regardless of participation)
- ❌ Rounds joined (doesn't count)
- ❌ Rounds cancelled (still counts)

**Maximizing value:**
- Create rounds with multiple spots
- Build regular playing groups
- Consider SUMMIT upgrade if needed

## Round Formats Explained

### Stroke Play

**How it works:**
- Count every stroke
- Lowest total score wins
- Most common format

**Best for:**
- Serious golfers
- Competitive rounds
- Handicap tracking

### Match Play

**How it works:**
- Hole-by-hole competition
- Win/lose/tie each hole
- Most holes won wins match

**Best for:**
- One-on-one competition
- Head-to-head challenges
- Exciting finishes

### Scramble

**How it works:**
- Teams of 2-4
- Everyone tees off
- Choose best shot, all play from there
- Repeat until holed

**Best for:**
- Mixed skill levels
- Corporate events
- Fun, social rounds

### Shamble

**How it works:**
- Everyone tees off
- Choose best drive
- Play own ball from there
- Individual scoring

**Best for:**
- Combination of team and individual
- Fair for different skill levels
- Social yet competitive

### Best Ball

**How it works:**
- Teams of 2-4
- Everyone plays own ball
- Lowest score per hole counts
- Team with lowest total wins

**Best for:**
- Team competition
- Mixed abilities
- Tournament play

## Best Practices

### For Round Creators

#### Do:
- **Arrive early**: Set the tone
- **Communicate clearly**: Tee times, costs, expectations
- **Be welcoming**: Include everyone
- **Have backup plan**: Weather, cancellations
- **Follow up**: Thank participants, plan next round

#### Don't:
- **Overbook**: Respect course pace
- **Exclude late**: Give people time to join
- **Change details last minute**: Disrupts planning
- **Forget to mark complete**: Affects reputation

### For Participants

#### Do:
- **Confirm promptly**: Don't leave organizer hanging
- **Arrive on time**: Respect others' time
- **Be prepared**: Clubs, balls, appropriate attire
- **Bring cash**: For any shared costs
- **Thank organizer**: They did the work

#### Don't:
- **No-show**: Seriously damages reputation
- **Cancel last minute**: Unless emergency
- **Be unprepared**: Slows down group
- **Forget payment**: Organizers cover costs

## Troubleshooting

### Can't Create Round

**Possible reasons:**
- FREE tier (upgrade required)
- SELECT tier limit reached (4/month)
- Profile incomplete

**Solutions:**
- Upgrade to SELECT/SUMMIT
- Wait for next month
- Complete profile

### Can't Join Round

**Possible reasons:**
- Round is full
- Round is private (need code)
- Different tier (for some private rounds)
- Round was cancelled

**Solutions:**
- Find another round
- Request invite code
- Join public rounds

### Need to Cancel

**As participant:**
1. Go to round details
2. Tap **"Leave Round"**
3. Confirm cancellation
4. Notify organizer via chat

**As organizer:**
1. Go to round details
2. Tap **"Cancel Round"**
3. Provide reason
4. All participants notified

**Reputation impact:**
- Cancelling 24+ hours before: No penalty
- Cancelling within 24 hours: Small penalty
- No-showing: Significant penalty

## FAQ

**Q: Can I edit round details after creation?**
A: Limited edits allowed (tee time, notes). Date/course cannot be changed. Cancel and recreate if needed.

**Q: What happens if I'm the organizer and can't make it?**
A: Cancel the round. All participants notified. Consider appointing a co-organizer beforehand.

**Q: How do I split costs with participants?**
A: Spotter doesn't handle payments. Arrange directly (cash, Venmo, etc.).

**Q: Can I join a round if I'm waitlisted?**
A: You'll be notified if a spot opens. Join quickly as spots fill fast.

**Q: What if the course cancels due to weather?**
A: Cancel the round in Spotter and notify participants. No penalty for weather cancellations.

**Q: Can I create recurring rounds?**
A: Not automatically. Create each round individually.
