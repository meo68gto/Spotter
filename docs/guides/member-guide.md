# Member Guide

Complete guide for Spotter members (FREE, SELECT, and SUMMIT tiers).

## Overview

As a Spotter member, you can:
- **Discover** golf rounds and connect with other golfers
- **Network** with members in your tier
- **Track** your golf stats and reputation
- **Book** coaching sessions (SELECT+)
- **Register** for tournaments (SELECT+)

## Getting Started

### 1. Sign Up

1. Download the Spotter app from the App Store
2. Open the app and tap "Get Started"
3. Enter your email address
4. Check your email for a 6-digit OTP code
5. Enter the code to complete signup
6. You'll be automatically assigned the **FREE** tier

### 2. Complete Your Profile

Your profile consists of three sections:

#### Basic Information (FREE)
- Display name
- Profile photo
- Bio
- City/Location
- Timezone

#### Professional Identity (SELECT+)
- Current role/title
- Company
- Industry
- LinkedIn URL
- Years of experience

#### Golf Identity (SELECT+)
- Handicap
- Home course
- Play frequency (weekly, biweekly, monthly, occasionally)
- Years playing
- Preferred tee times

### 3. Build Your Network

Spotter uses **same-tier visibility** - you can only see and connect with members in your tier.

#### FREE Tier Network Features
- View up to **20** other members per search
- Maximum **50** connections
- Send connection requests
- Accept/decline incoming requests

#### SELECT Tier Network Features
- Unlimited search results
- Maximum **500** connections
- Send introduction requests via mutual connections
- Priority placement in search results

#### SUMMIT Tier Network Features
- Unlimited connections
- Priority Boosts (appear higher in searches)
- Advanced filtering and matching
- Direct messaging with any same-tier member

## Using the App

### Home Tab

Your dashboard shows:
- Upcoming rounds
- Connection requests
- Recommended members to connect with
- Recent activity

### Golf Tab

Discover and join rounds:
1. Browse available rounds in your area
2. Filter by date, course, format, or spots available
3. Tap a round to see details
4. Tap "Join Round" to request a spot

**For SELECT+ members:**
- Create new rounds by tapping "+"
- Set round details (course, date, format, spots)
- Choose public (same-tier visibility) or private (invite-only)

### Network Tab

Manage your connections:
- **Connections**: See your current connections
- **Requests**: View pending incoming/outgoing requests
- **Discover**: Find new members to connect with
- **Search**: Filter by location, handicap, company, etc.

**Tips for building your network:**
- Complete your profile for better visibility
- Add a personal message when sending requests
- Connect with people you've actually played with
- Give introductions to help others connect

### Profile Tab

Manage your account:
- Edit profile information
- View reputation score
- Check tier status
- Access settings
- Upgrade tier (FREE/SELECT only)

## Reputation System

Your reputation score (0-100) is calculated from:

| Component | Weight | How It's Calculated |
|-----------|--------|---------------------|
| Profile Completeness | 10% | Percentage of profile filled |
| Completion Rate | 15% | Percentage of rounds completed (no no-shows) |
| Ratings Average | 25% | Average ratings from other players |
| Network Size | 20% | Number of connections |
| Referrals | 15% | Successful referrals made |
| Attendance Rate | 10% | On-time arrival percentage |

### Reputation Events

Actions that affect your score:

**Positive:**
- Complete a round (+5 points)
- Receive positive rating (+10 points)
- Make a successful referral (+15 points)
- Complete your profile (+20 points)

**Negative:**
- No-show for a round (-20 points)
- Cancel late (-10 points)
- Receive negative rating (-15 points)
- Decline connections frequently (-5 points)

## Tips for Success

### Getting the Most from FREE Tier

1. **Complete your basic profile** - Even limited info helps with connections
2. **Be selective with connections** - Quality over quantity with 50-connection limit
3. **Join public rounds** - Meet new people and expand organically
4. **Attend events** - Many events are open to all tiers

### Maximizing SELECT Tier

1. **Fill out professional identity** - Great for business networking
2. **Create regular rounds** - Build a consistent playing group
3. **Use all 4 monthly rounds** - That's 16-20 new connections per month
4. **Send introduction requests** - Help others connect and boost reputation

### Summit Tier Advantages

1. **Use Priority Boosts** - Get seen first in searches
2. **Create unlimited rounds** - Build a thriving golf community
3. **Take advantage of early registration** - Secure spots in popular events
4. **Network with Summit members** - Connect with serious golfers

## Troubleshooting

### "Tier visibility restricted" error

This means you're trying to view a profile from a different tier. You can only see members in your tier or higher tiers. Upgrade your tier to see more members.

### Can't create rounds

Round creation is a SELECT+ feature. Upgrade to create your own rounds.

### Connection limit reached

FREE tier: Remove some connections to add new ones.
SELECT tier: You have 500 connections available.

### Reputation score not updating

Reputation recalculates every 24 hours. Recent changes won't reflect immediately.

## FAQ

**Q: Can I change tiers?**
A: Yes, you can upgrade at any time. See the [Tier Upgrade Guide](./tier-upgrade.md).

**Q: What happens if I downgrade?**
A: You'll keep existing connections but can't add new ones if over the limit.

**Q: Can I see members in higher tiers?**
A: Yes, tier visibility works one-way: you can see your tier and higher tiers.

**Q: How do I earn reputation points?**
A: Complete rounds, get positive ratings, make referrals, and keep your profile updated.

**Q: Can I message anyone?**
A: You can message connections. SUMMIT members can message same-tier members directly.
