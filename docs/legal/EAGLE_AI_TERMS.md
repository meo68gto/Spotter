## 2. Description of Eagle AI

Eagle AI is Spotter's AI-powered golf coaching engine. It analyzes your golf swing (via video capture and pose keypoint extraction) and generates personalized coaching content including:

- Swing tips and corrective cues
- Practice drill recommendations
- Practice plan suggestions
- On-course strategy advice

Eagle AI operates using a multi-axis self-verification system ("EAGLE-Coach") that runs AI-generated coaching content through safety, factual, biomechanical, and citation checks before delivering output to you.

## 5. Safety Verifier — "AI Reviewed for Safety"

### 5.1 Multi-Axis Safety Verification System

Eagle AI uses a multi-axis safety verification system (the EAGLE-Coach self-verification loop) before delivering any coaching recommendation. This system includes:

| Verifier | What It Checks |
|----------|----------------|
| **V1 — Factual Consistency** | Does the tip address the detected swing fault? |
| **V2 — Biomechanical Validity** | Is the cue mechanically sound for the detected swing pattern? |
| **V3 — Safety/Appropriateness** | Is the drill appropriate for the user's skill level and physical condition? |
| **V4 — Internal Consistency** | Does the cue logically follow from the observed pose and fault data? |
| **V5 — Citation** | Does the recommendation reference a verified drill in the Spotter drill library? |

If any verifier fails, Eagle AI runs a repair loop to refine the output before delivery.

### 5.2 Safety Is a Feature, Not a Liability Limitation

The multi-axis safety verification system is Spotter's investment in delivering responsible AI coaching. This is marketed as **"AI reviewed for safety"** — meaning every Eagle AI recommendation has passed through V1–V5 checks before reaching you.

However, the existence of this system does not:
- Create a warranty or guarantee regarding the safety or accuracy of any recommendation
- Limit Spotter's liability below what applicable law requires
- Constitute a representation that Eagle AI is error-free or suitable for all users
