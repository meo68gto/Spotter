## 2. The Service Description

Spotter is a golf networking platform that enables serious amateur golfers to find, connect with, and schedule rounds with other golfers of comparable skill levels. The Service includes:

- **User profiles** with skill-tier classification (handicap-based matching)
- **Discovery and matching** between users within the same skill tier
- **Round scheduling** and coordination tools
- **Golf course discovery** and directory
- **Coaching marketplace** (Spotter SELECT+ tier)
- **Organizer tools** for tournament and event management (Spotter Organizer tiers)
- **Video analysis** tools for performance improvement
- **Video-based content** sharing and social features

The Service is available via web, iOS, and Android applications.
