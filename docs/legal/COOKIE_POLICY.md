## 1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device (computer, smartphone, tablet) when you visit a website or use an application. They are widely used to make websites and applications work more efficiently, provide a better user experience, and give website owners useful information.

**Types of cookies:**

| Type | Description |
|------|-------------|
| **Session cookies** | Temporary cookies that are deleted when you close your browser or app |
| **Persistent cookies** | Cookies that remain on your device for a set period or until manually deleted |
| **First-party cookies** | Cookies set by the website or app you are visiting directly |
| **Third-party cookies** | Cookies set by third-party services embedded in or linked from the website (e.g., analytics, advertising) |

## 5. Managing and Disabling Cookies

### 5.1 Via Our Consent Manager

You can update your cookie preferences at any time by:
- Clicking "Cookie Settings" in our website footer
- Going to **Settings → Privacy → Cookie Preferences** in the mobile app

### 5.2 Via Your Browser

Most browsers allow you to control cookies through their settings. Common options:

| Browser | How to Manage Cookies |
|---------|----------------------|
| **Safari** | Preferences → Privacy → Cookies and website data |
| **Chrome** | Settings → Privacy and security → Cookies |
| **Firefox** | Options → Privacy & Security → Cookies |
| **Edge** | Settings → Privacy, search, and services → Cookies |

**Note:** Disabling cookies via your browser will apply to all websites and may affect functionality.

### 5.3 Via Your Mobile Device

**iOS:** Go to Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking → Turn off "Allow Apps to Request to Track"

**Android:** Go to Settings → Privacy → Ads → Opt out of personalized ads

**Note:** These device-level settings control advertising identifiers, not HTTP cookies specifically.

### 5.4 Opt-Out Links for Analytics Providers

- **PostHog:** [https://posthog.com/privacy](https://posthog.com/privacy) — PostHog supports an opt-out mechanism
- **Google Analytics:** [https://tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout](https://tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout)
