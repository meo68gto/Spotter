## 5. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect your information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction, including:

### 6.4 Account Deletion Process

To delete your account:
1. Go to **Settings → Account → Delete Account** in the Spotter app
2. Or email deletion@spotter.golf with your request
3. We will verify your identity before processing the deletion
