# Research Memo: Strava + Hinge + ClassPass Patterns

## Key Patterns
- Strava: Social graph driven by activity artifacts; habit loops built on progress visibility.
- Hinge: Intentional matching prioritizing quality and constrained choices over feed volume.
- ClassPass: Supply-side aggregation with trust and standardization across fragmented providers.

## Spotter Implications
- Make activity/session records first-class social objects.
- Limit match presentation to top 3-5 candidates to drive decision quality.
- Build coach marketplace trust primitives early (ratings, specialization tags, transparent pricing).

## Technical Translation
- Rank on compatibility and expected session quality, not popularity.
- Preserve timeline of improvement (progress snapshots) as retention anchor.
- Keep taxonomy extensible for new activities and local market variants.
